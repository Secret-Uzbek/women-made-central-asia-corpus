# Primary Corpus

Women are not marginal supplements to political history. They are operators of continuity.

To prove that seriously, the project needs a corpus that captures deep history, dynastic power, sacred transition, household transmission, material culture, trade and adornment, oral memory, and literary imagination.

## Backbone
- Starr, Lost Enlightenment
- A Chronology of Central Asia
- Country of a Thousand Cities
- Masson, First Civilizations
- Bartold, Turks
- Buddhism and Islam in Eastern Turkestan
- Sogdian silver
- trade domes
- cuisine and domestic continuity
